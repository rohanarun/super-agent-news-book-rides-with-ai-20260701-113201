How booking rides with AI works

1) Ask for a ride

Tell Super your destination, timing, and preferences (quiet ride, curbside pickup, accessibility). Super assists — you review before confirming.

2) Compare options

Super checks available apps and surfaces ETAs and estimates so you can choose. This helps counter dynamic pricing differences reported by Consumer Reports.

3) Confirm & coordinate

Pickup notes, gate codes, and arrival messages are coordinated. You stay in control of final confirmation.

4) Reuse your routes

Frequent trips are remembered using a computer-use cache, so repeated workflows don’t start from scratch.
